Writing Descriptive Text - Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is an American sitcom-star who turned into a famous pop singer. She is known for hit songs such as ‘Problem’, ‘Bang Bang Bang’ and ‘Thank U, Next’

Ariana was born in Boca Raton, Florida in 1993 and began her career since she was a child.

Ariana Grande has been nominated for six Grammy Awards and she won three American Music Awards, including Artist of the Year in 2016 and two MTV Video Music Awards.

She is well known for being a good looking singer. Her iconic style is wearing mini skirt or dress during on her performance/show. Ariana’s signature hairdo is high ponytail.

Pembahasan
