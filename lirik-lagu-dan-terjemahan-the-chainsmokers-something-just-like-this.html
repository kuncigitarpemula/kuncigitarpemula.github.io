20. Please analyze 10 of the lyrics below from the song "Something Just Like This" from Coldplay and The Chainsmokers And tell me your reason why these sentence represents the SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE 1.I don't see myself upon that list 2. How much you wanna risk? 3. I want something just like this 4. Superman unrolls 5. he lifts 6. I'm not the kind of person 7. it fits 8. I want something just like this 9. I want something just like this 10. How much you wanna risk?


Jawaban:

1.I don't see myself upon that list

Reasons: The sentence follows the negative form of Simple Present Tense = (-) Subject + Do/Does + not + Verb-1 + Object

2. How much you wanna risk?

Reasons: The sentence follows the interrogative form of Simple Present Tense = (?) 5W1H (How much) + Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

3. I want something just like this

Reasons: The sentence follows the Positive form of Simple Present Tense = (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

4. Superman unrolls

Reasons: The sentence follows the Positive form of Simple Present Tense = (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

5. he lifts

Reasons: The sentence follows the Positive form of Simple Present Tense = (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

6. I'm not the kind of person

Reasons: The sentence follows the negative form of Simple Present Tense = Nominal: (-) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

7. it fits

Reasons: The sentence follows the Positive form of Simple Present Tense = (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

8. I want something just like this

Reasons: The sentence follows the Positive form of Simple Present Tense = (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

9. I want something just like this

Reasons: The sentence follows the Positive form of Simple Present Tense = (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

10. How much you wanna risk?

Reasons: The sentence follows the interrogative form of Simple Present Tense = (?) 5W1H (How much) + Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban berdasarkan teks pada soal yang diambil dari sebuah lirik lagu terkenal dari ColdPlay and The Chainsmokers khususnya bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan fakta dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Alasan utamanya sederhana, semua kalimat mengikuti Formula (rumus) dari bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense.

Simple Present Tense:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

(-) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + Subject + Adjective / Noun / Adverb + ?

Verbal: (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

(-) Subject + Do/Does + not + Verb-1 + Object

(?) Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

(?) 5W1H (What) + Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?
