4. 20 kalimat would you like to beserta terjemahannya


1. "Would you like to dance?"
"Maukah kau menari?"

2. "Would you like to drink a cup of tea?"
"Maukah kau minum secangkir teh?"

3. "Would you like to play?"
"Maukah kau bermain?"

4. "Would you like to come?"
"Maukah kau datang?"

5. "Would you like to go to the party with me?"
"Maukah kau pergi ke pesta denganku?"

6. "Would you like to learn Latin?"
"Maukah kau belajar Latin?"

7. "Would you like to study in the library?"
"Maukah kau belajar di perpustakaan?"

8. "Would you like to listen to instrumental songs?"
"Maukah kau mendengarkan lagu-lagu instrumental?"

9. "Would you like to go to the park?"
"Maukah kau pergi ke taman?"

10. "Would you like to study Math?"
"Maukah kau belajar matematika?"

Dapat dilihat dari beberapa contoh diatas, bahwa membuat kalimat "Would you like to...?" tidaklah susah, setelah segmen kalimat itu tambahkanlah kata kerja (verb, dan biasanya verb pertama.) dan jika dibutuhkan, kata benda yang membantu atau melengkapi. Begitu juga terjemahannya. Pada dasarnya "Would you like to...?" dapat diterjemahkan menjadi "Maukah kau/kamu/engkau/anda...?", terjemahan selanjutnya mengikuti kalimat inggris yang dibuat.

Untuk 10 contoh sisanya coba buat sendiri ya ＾＾

10. terjemahan where Will the meeting be hold​


Jawaban:

Mungkin, kalimat bahasa inggris yang lebih tepat: Where will the meeting be held?

Artinya: Di mana pertemuan itu akan diadakan?

Penjelasan:

Where = di mana

The meeting = pertemuan

will be held = akan diadakan

will + be + V3 merupakan bentuk kalimat pasif (passive voice) pada future tense

Pada kalimat pasif digunakan V3, bukan V1 sehingga hold (V1) menjadi held (V3)

29. If you want to check your CD how should you hold the CD


Jawaban:

When handling a CD (Compact Disc), it's important to hold it properly to avoid any damage. Here are some tips:

1. **Hold by the Edges**: Always hold a CD by its outer edge. Never touch the shiny, reflective surface with your fingers as it can leave smudges and fingerprints that can interfere with the disc's readability.

2. **Use the Center Hole**: You can also put your finger through the center hole of the CD for a more secure grip.

3. **Avoid Scratches**: Be careful not to scratch the disc. Even small scratches can cause skips or prevent the CD from playing entirely.

4. **Keep it Clean**: If you need to clean the CD, wipe it gently with a soft, lint-free cloth from the center of the disc straight out to the edge. Do not wipe in a circular motion as this could cause more damage.

5. **Storage**: When you're not using the CD, store it in its case to protect it from dust and scratches.

Remember, improper handling can cause permanent damage to a CD, affecting its longevity and performance.
